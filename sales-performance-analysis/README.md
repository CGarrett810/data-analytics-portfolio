## Business Problem
Leadership wanted to understand which regions and product categories were performing well and which were underperforming.

## Objective
Identify sales patterns across regions and product categories and provide actionable recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Python (pandas)
- pandas
- EDA
- Excel (initial review)

## Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicate records
- Standardized region and category values
- Converted sales to numeric values
- Converted order dates to datetime format
- Removed records with missing critical fields

## Analysis Performed
- Total sales by region
- Total sales by product category

## Key Insights
- The West region is the highest-performing region
- The South region is the lowest-performing region
- Furniture is the top-selling category
- Office Supplies is the lowest-selling category

## Recommendations
- Replicate successful strategies from the West region across underperforming regions
- Implement targeted promotions to improve Office Supplies sales
